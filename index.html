Frequently asked questions

What is lifeHRMS?

+

lifeHRMS is a comprehensive cloud-based Human Resource Management System that handles payroll, attendance, leave management, performance reviews, recruitment, onboarding, and employee self-service — all in one unified platform by Leanmo Software.

How long does it take to set up?

+

Most companies are fully operational within a day. Basic configuration takes under 15 minutes, and employee onboarding can be done via bulk CSV import or self-registration.

Is there a free trial?

+

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial on the Standard plan with no credit card required. Experience the full power of lifeHRMS before committing.

Can lifeHRMS scale with my company?

+

Absolutely. lifeHRMS is designed for organizations from 10 to 10,000+ employees. Our modular architecture means you only pay for what you use, and you can add modules as your needs evolve.

Is my data secure?

+

Security is our top priority. lifeHRMS uses bank-grade encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, role-based access controls, and regular security audits to keep your data safe.

Do you offer customer support?

+

Yes, we provide dedicated customer support via email, chat, and phone. Premium plan customers get a dedicated account manager and priority support SLA.
